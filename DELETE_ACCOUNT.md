# Account Deletion Request - ShopSmart

We provide a simple way for users to manage and delete their accounts and associated data.

## How to Delete Your Account
You can delete your account and all associated data using either of the following methods:

### 1. Within the App (Recommended)
1. Open the **ShopSmart** app.
2. Navigate to **Settings** or your **Profile** section.
3. Select **Delete Account**.
4. Confirm your choice. Your account, profile information, and all shop-related data (including customers, inventory, and bills) will be permanently removed from our servers immediately.

### 2. Request via Email
If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:
- **Email**: smart.shop.contact.apk@gmail.com
- **Subject**: Account Deletion Request - [Your Email/User ID]
- **Processing Time**: We will process your request and delete all data within 7 business days.

## Data Deletion & Retention
When you delete your account, the following data is permanently wiped from our secure database (Supabase):
- **Identity Information**: Name, Email, and Profile Picture.
- **Business Data**: Your Shop profile, Inventory records, and Product list.
- **Transaction History**: All Bills, Sales records, and Payment history.
- **Staff Details**: Any links between your shop and staff members.

**Note**: Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered. We do not retain any of your personal or business data after the account is deleted, except where required by law (e.g., for tax or regulatory compliance).
